    How Goal Quest works

    Long-haul goals become a chronicle of small, playable wins.

    01

    Design a quest

    Every goal becomes a 100-point quest. Break it into milestones — 20 points for a week of reading, 40 for the half-way mark — and promise yourself a small reward at each one.

    02

    Log the work

    Record actions as you do them — pages read, kilometres run, euros saved. The bar fills, the chest unlocks, your deeds are etched into the chronicle for good.

    03

    Climb the ranks

    Every point you log is XP in the Hall of Fame. Measure yourself against the realm's champions and keep the streak alive.

      How it works

      Each quest is worth 100 points. Define milestones from 1 to 99 points, each with a reward. Log every action — the bar fills, the chest opens. Your chronicle lives in the browser.

      Themes

      Parchment, Tavern, Elven Forest, Frozen Realm. Each with light and dark variants. The palette picker on the top right switches on the fly.

      Data

      Everything is stored locally in your browser. No account, no backend. Use Ctrl+Shift+E to export a JSON backup anytime.
