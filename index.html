      How it works

      Each quest is worth 100 points. Define milestones from 1 to 99 points, each with a reward. Log every action — the bar fills, the chest opens. Your chronicle lives in the browser.

      Themes

      Parchment, Tavern, Elven Forest, Frozen Realm. Each with light and dark variants. The palette picker on the top right switches on the fly.

      Data

      Everything is stored locally in your browser. No account, no backend. Use Ctrl+Shift+E to export a JSON backup anytime.
